Our Story

Joyalty Photography was founded by Steve Moses in Shanzu, Mombasa — where golden-hour light meets the Indian Ocean and every celebration carries the warmth of coastal culture.

What began as a passion for capturing candid family moments evolved into a full-service studio trusted by couples, brands and families across Kenya. We believe every photograph should feel like a painting — composed with intention, lit with feeling, delivered with craft.

Today, Joyalty operates as a registered Kenyan business committed to professional excellence, transparent client relationships and creative storytelling that stands the test of time.

— Steve Moses, CEO

Refund & Rescheduling Policy
Effective July 2026  ·  11 sections  ·  Kenya Consumer Protection Act 2012

This policy sets out the terms under which bookings may be cancelled, refunded or rescheduled. All refunds are calculated based on the Planning Stage reached at the time of written cancellation. Cancellations must be submitted in writing to joyaltyphotography254@gmail.com — verbal cancellations are not valid.

Cancellation scenario Refund amount
Cancel >72 hrs — Minimal planning stage 100% less deposit & 5% admin fee
Cancel >72 hrs — Early planning stage 75% of total fees
Cancel >72 hrs — Halfway planning stage 50% of total fees
Cancel >72 hrs — Advanced planning stage 25% or no refund
Cancel within 72 hours of event No refund — full fees due
No-show / late arrival / client default No refund — full fees immediately due
Photographer cancels (non-force majeure) Full refund within 14 business days
